Current Projects
Generalized Locomotion Controllers for Legged Robots
September 2022 - Present
Developing a morphology-agnostic controller that can achieve zero-shot generalized locomotion on any legged robot including A1, Cassie, Ant, Humanoid, etc.


Generative Models for Hadronization
February 2023 - Present
Developing a generative network to replace the hadronization pipeline in High Energy Physics collision simulators like Herwig, Pythia, etc.


Investigating Control Methods for Northeastern's Aerobat MAV
December 2023 - Present
Training robust RL, PID policies to control the Aerobat MAV with flapping wing dynamics. Biggest limitation is terrible wind mechanics in simulators, making simulations and trajectory collection difficult.
